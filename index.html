How we work

Design thinking, applied to putting the right tool in your team's hands.

  • 01

    Listen

    A half-day on-site, no forms, no slide decks — just a conversation about where time actually gets lost in the day.

  • 02

    Map

    We lay out the real workflow together and identify which AI product actually fits — not the one everyone's talking about, but the one that fits how your team already works.

  • 03

    Pilot

    A small, cheap, reversible test — run hands-on by your own team, with us alongside, so the confidence to use it sits with them from day one.

  • 04

    Enable

    We train the team properly, write it down simply, and make sure they can run it themselves — not lean on us to keep it going.

  • 05

    Review

    We sit down again after 90 days and are honest about what's working, what isn't, and whether the tool itself still fits.

Common questions

Straight answers, before you get in touch.

Will AI replace my staff?

No. Every engagement is built around a human-in-the-loop model — AI supports a task, a person stays in control of the outcome. The goal is freeing up time, not removing people.

How much does it cost?

A fixed fee, agreed before any work starts. There are no hidden charges, no annual contracts, and no surprise invoices.

What if AI isn't the right fit for my business?

Then we say so plainly. Part of the process is finding out honestly whether AI is the right answer at all, not selling a tool regardless of fit.

Do I need any technical knowledge to work with SeaGlass?

No. The process is designed for business owners with no technical background, and everything is explained in plain language.
