How we work

Design thinking, applied to finding the right use case — not the flashiest one.

  • 01

    Listen

    A half-day on-site, no forms, no slide decks — just a conversation about where time actually gets lost in the day.

  • 02

    Map

    We lay out the real workflow together and find the one or two points where AI could genuinely help — and just as importantly, where it can't.

  • 03

    Pilot

    A small, cheap, reversible test with your team, on your real work — not a demo, not a slide deck.

  • 04

    Embed

    We train the team, write it down simply, and make sure it keeps working once we're not in the room.

  • 05

    Review

    We sit down again after 90 days and are honest about what's working, what isn't, and what happens next.
