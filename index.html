How we work

Design thinking, applied to putting the right tool in your team's hands.

Every SeaGlass Digital engagement follows the same five-step AI adoption process — Listen, Map, Pilot, Enable, Review — built specifically for small and medium UK businesses. It's the same structured approach used to introduce AI safely at HSBC and Diageo, scaled down to a size and cost that makes sense for a five-person shop rather than a global enterprise.

01

Listen

A half-day on-site, no forms, no slide decks — just a conversation about where time actually gets lost in the day, and what's already working.

→
02

Map

We lay out the real workflow together and identify which AI product actually fits — not the one everyone's talking about, but the one that fits how your team already works.

→
03

Pilot

A small, cheap, reversible test — run hands-on by your own team, with us alongside, so the confidence to use it sits with them from day one, not with us.

→
04

Enable

We train the team properly, write it down simply, and make sure they can run it themselves — not lean on us to keep it going once the project ends.

→
05

Review

We sit down again after 90 days and are honest about what's working, what isn't, and whether the tool itself still fits as your business changes.
