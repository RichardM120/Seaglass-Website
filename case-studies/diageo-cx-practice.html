Turning a 4-person team into a 40-agency global practice

Diageo · Global CX/UX Practice, 2018–2025

Design and user experience work was happening, but inconsistently — different agencies, different standards, different quality, and no shared way of measuring whether any of it was actually working for customers.

The brief

Build a genuine practice — not a single project — that could set a consistent standard for design thinking and UX across dozens of external agencies and markets, starting from a team of four.

The approach

  • Established a shared design-thinking methodology that any agency partner could be briefed into, rather than reinventing process per project
  • Shifted the practice's role from purely executing design work to shaping strategy earlier, before briefs were locked
  • Standardised how success was measured, so 40+ agencies were working towards the same definition of "good"

Why it matters for a smaller business

Most local businesses aren't managing 40 agencies — but they are often juggling a website person, a social media freelancer, an accountant, and now an AI tool, all pulling in different directions with no shared plan.
